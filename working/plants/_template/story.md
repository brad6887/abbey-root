# Plant Story

This document contains the public-facing narrative for the plant.

It should explain why the plant's story is worth reading without repeating the full chronological detail from `history.md`.

## Purpose

Use this file to tell the plant's story in a concise, readable way.

The narrative may include:

- How the plant entered the collection
- Why it stood out
- Major setbacks or turning points
- Important moves or environmental changes
- What made the rescue or growth process meaningful
- What the plant represents now

## Writing Guidance

- Write in Brad's natural voice.
- Keep the tone conversational.
- Prefer a clear story over a care guide.
- Use `history.md` as the factual reference.
- Do not invent details or dates.
- Avoid duplicating structured facts from `facts.yaml` unless they help the story.
- Keep the published narrative shorter than the full history.
