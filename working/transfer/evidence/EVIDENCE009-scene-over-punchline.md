# Evidence – Observation 009 – Scene Over Punchline

## Observation

Observation 009 proposes that the author tends to present complete scenes or situations rather than building toward an explicit punchline.

**Date:** 2009-07-23

**Score:** +2

**Post:**

**Analysis:**
