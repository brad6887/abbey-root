# Observation 001

## Question

Does the corpus accurately represent natural opening sentence structure?

## Findings

No, at least for early Facebook posts.

Many early Facebook status updates were written to complete Facebook's
"Brad Cooke is..." prompt.

Examples:

- is rebooting
- is confused
- is drinking coffee
- is sneezing

These openings are artifacts of the Facebook interface rather than
independent stylistic choices.

## Implication

Opening-sentence analysis should exclude or normalize these early
status-style posts.
