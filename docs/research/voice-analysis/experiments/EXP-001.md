# Experiment

## Objective

Validate the Voice Analysis methodology using a normalized Facebook corpus and establish a reproducible baseline for future writing analysis experiments.

## Research Question

Can the Voice Analysis methodology transform a normalized writing corpus into traceable observations, evidence, hypotheses, and validation results while remaining reproducible?

## Scope

Source:

- Facebook HTML Archive

Corpus:

- CORPUS-001

The experiment focuses on the initial application of the methodology to a normalized Facebook writing corpus.

The experiment is not intended to produce a complete Voice Model.

## Corpus

The experiment uses CORPUS-001.

Corpus characteristics:

- Parsed posts: 3,039
- Clean posts: 1,752
- Excluded posts: 1,287

Normalization was performed using:

- scripts/build_clean_corpus.py

The frozen corpus fingerprint is documented in CORPUS-001.

The experiment applies the Voice Analysis lifecycle:

Observation

↓

↓

The experiment evaluates the practicality and reproducibility of each stage.
