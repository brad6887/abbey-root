# Case Study 001 – Brad Cooke

## Objective

Develop and validate an evidence-based methodology for understanding an author's writing voice.

Brad Cooke serves as the first case study due to the availability of a large corpus spanning multiple decades, writing styles, and subject areas.

## Research Log

### 2026-07-17

Initial observations:

- Voice appears remarkably stable across seventeen years.
- Structure evolves more than voice.
- Humor is frequently observational.
- Technical writing increasingly adopts narrative structure.
- Author frequently trusts readers to infer meaning.

These observations are provisional and require supporting evidence.
