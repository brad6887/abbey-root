# Quality Standards

## Purpose

This document defines the quality standards expected for research conducted using the Voice Analysis framework.

These standards promote consistency, credibility, and long-term value across all research projects.

---

# Research Standards

## Completeness

Research should fully address the stated objective.

Major conclusions should be supported by sufficient evidence.

Known gaps should be documented.

---

## Accuracy

Research artifacts should accurately represent the source material.

Quotations, references, and interpretations should remain faithful to the original corpus.

---

## Traceability

Every significant conclusion should be traceable to:

- Source material
- Observations
- Evidence
- Validated hypotheses

Readers should be able to reconstruct the reasoning process.

---

## Consistency

Terminology, methodology, and documentation should remain consistent throughout the project.

The same concepts should be described using the same language whenever practical.

---

## Reproducibility

Another researcher following the methodology should be able to reach substantially similar conclusions from the same corpus.

---

## Transparency

Research decisions, assumptions, limitations, and revisions should be documented throughout the project.

---

## Validation

Major conclusions should undergo structured validation before becoming part of the Voice Model.

Validation should include the search for contradictory evidence where appropriate.

---

## Documentation

Research artifacts should be complete, organized, and understandable without requiring undocumented context.

---

## Versioning

Significant revisions should be documented so the evolution of research remains understandable over time.

---

## Stewardship

Research should be preserved as a long-term knowledge asset.

Documentation should remain useful to future researchers.

---

# Reviewing Research

Researchers should periodically evaluate completed work against these standards.

Questions to consider include:

- Is every conclusion supported?
- Can the research be reproduced?
- Are limitations documented?
- Is the reasoning easy to follow?
- Would another researcher understand this work?

---

# Success Criteria

Research meeting these standards should be:

- Credible
- Reproducible
- Maintainable
- Understandable
- Useful for future research
