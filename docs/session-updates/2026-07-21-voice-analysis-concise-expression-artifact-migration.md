## Research Finding

The migrated observation was refined from "Preference for Concise Writing" into "Preference for Concise Expression."

The formal observation focuses on the author's tendency to communicate complete ideas using minimal language while relying on context and reader inference.

## Hypothesis

HYP-002 proposes that the author frequently compresses ideas by relying on shared context and reader inference rather than explicitly explaining every detail.

The hypothesis remains:

- Status: Proposed
- Confidence: Low

Additional validation is required across different writing formats and time periods.

## Validation

VAL-002 evaluated:

- Time period diversity
- Writing format diversity
- Counterexamples

Current outcome:

- Provisionally Supported

The available evidence supports the hypothesis within the limited scope of the first 100 chronological Facebook posts.
