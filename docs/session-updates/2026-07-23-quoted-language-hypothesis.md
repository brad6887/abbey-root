# Quoted Language Hypothesis

## Objective

Turn OBS-004 and EVID-004 into a narrow, falsifiable hypothesis with an
independent validation population and explicit thresholds.

## Work Completed

Created HYP-004:

```text
Quotation Marks Function as Stance Markers
```

The hypothesis is limited to quote-bearing authored Facebook posts. Its core
claim covers:

- scare or distancing quotation,
- and invented labels or comic renaming.

Ordinary titles, neutral quotations, reported speech, copied text, and
malformed punctuation do not count as core support.

## Independent Validation Design

VAL-004 must start from the deterministic 165-candidate set and remove:

- 15 canonical supporting examples,
- 7 canonical comparisons,
- and 4 rejected matches.

The remaining holdout is constructed by exact source identifier.

## Predictions

The holdout must contain:

- at least 30% core supporting cases,
- at least 10 distancing cases,
- at least 10 renaming cases,
- core cases in at least three of four chronological bands,
- and at least 20 comparison cases.

Failure of either subtype requires narrowing the hypothesis even if the
aggregate rate passes.

## Artifact State

```text
OBS-004 -> EVID-004 -> HYP-004
```

VAL-004 remains intentionally absent.

## Suggested Next Step

Build VAL-004 by deriving the exact holdout set from REVIEW-004 and EVID-004,
then calculate every threshold deterministically.
