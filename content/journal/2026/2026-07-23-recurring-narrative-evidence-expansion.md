# Recurring Narrative Evidence Expansion

## Summary

Voice Analysis expanded the recurring-narrative hypothesis from one 2009 time-machine sequence to five human-reviewed clusters spanning 2009 through 2024.

## Method Lesson

The evidence unit for recurrence is a relationship between posts.

A repeated word, topic, real event, organization, or platform pattern is not enough. Later writing must continue, modify, recall, or assume familiarity with a distinctive earlier element.

Model retrieval discovered candidates, deterministic search expanded them, and human review rejected false recurrence.

## Result

Retained clusters include the time machine, overheard-at-work frame, pay-it-forward inversion, GrandBrad persona, and annual National Daughters Day failure.

EVID-003 and VAL-003 were revised to Version 2. The hypothesis remains provisionally supported with medium confidence inside Facebook writing.

## Integrity Lesson

One model citation used the wrong identifier, one run exhausted its output budget, and one returned an empty length-limited result.

Review also found thirty-three residual `Mobile uploads Place:` records in the unflagged batches. They were excluded manually, but the eligibility rule should be considered for a future correction.
