# Project Journal

This journal documents the ongoing evolution of my projects.

Rather than only publishing finished results, I use these entries to capture progress, lessons learned, design decisions, and the occasional mistake along the way.

Many of the ideas documented here eventually become permanent features, documentation, or project pages.

## What You'll Find

Journal entries may include:

- Weekly progress updates
- Architecture decisions
- Lessons learned
- Hardware changes
- Automation experiments
- AI workflows
- Website improvements
- Infrastructure upgrades

The goal isn't to present perfect projects.

The goal is to document continuous improvement.
